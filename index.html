Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website?

Depending on the package: Starter takes 3-4 days, Professional takes 5-7 days, and Premium takes 7-10 days. These are real timelines — I work fast without compromising quality.

Do you build websites in Arabic?

Absolutely! I'm a native Arabic speaker and specialize in bilingual (Arabic/English) websites with proper RTL layout. Your Arabic content will look natural and professional, not like a translated afterthought.

What do I need to provide?

Just your business information, logo (if you have one — I can design one for +$50), any text content, and photos. If you don't have photos, I can source professional ones. We'll discuss everything via WhatsApp.

Do you provide hosting and domain?

Yes! For an additional $50/year, I can set up hosting and register a domain for your business. I'll also handle all the technical setup so you don't have to worry about anything.

Can I make changes after the website is delivered?

Yes — every package comes with 30 days of free support for minor changes. For ongoing updates, maintenance plans start at $30/month.

How do I pay?

I accept payment via bank transfer, Wise, PayPal, or local payment methods for Algeria. Payment is typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery.
